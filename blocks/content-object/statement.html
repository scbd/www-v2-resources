UN Publishes Negotiation Proposals for Sharing Benefits from Plant, Animal, Microbial DNA Sequences to Support Countries and Communities Protecting Nature
Montreal, Canada
UN documents released today provide proposals and options to meet a global pledge to share fairly some of the multi-trillion-dollar revenues and other benefits derived from products produced, improved or related to the use of "digital sequence information on genetic resources" such as the DNA sequences of plants, animals and microbes.
